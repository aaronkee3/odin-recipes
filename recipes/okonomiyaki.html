Recipe of Okonomiyaki

To make good okonomiyaki, it should use alot of oil to make it crispy.

Other fillings can enhance the taste of the Okonomiyaki

Ingredients:

  • Four
  • Water
  • Cabbage
  • Baking powder
  • Oil

    • Steps:

      1) Mix 60g flour, 30ml water, 1g baking powder and 1 large cabbage
      2) Lightly oil the pan and turn up the heat
      3) Place the mix into the frying pan
